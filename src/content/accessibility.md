## Ebook Accessibility
*Last manual evaluation: December 2025* 

It is the goal of Lever Press that every title we publish follows [WCAG AA](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/) and [EPUB Accessibility](https://www.w3.org/TR/epub-a11y-11/) compliance standards. 

 Frontlist EPUBs conform to WCAG 2.1 AA and EPUB Accessibility 1.1. EPUBs published prior to 2024 may conform to older versions of WCAG AA and EPUB Accessibility and for some titles conformance to accessibility requirements varies. In Fall 2025 we launched a remediation project to identify and fix all backlist titles failing to meet accessibility requirements.

 The EPUB format is available for online reading and download through the Fulcrum Reader. The PDF format is made available for download-only. PDF formats have varying levels of accessibility conformance, most notably PDF formats may not contain textual descriptions of images but do have baseline accessibility features and will work with assistive technology. All titles we publish are open access, can be freely downloaded, and do not contain DRM restrictions.

Published content is evaluated using a combination of automated tools and manual processes, primarily [Ace by DAISY](https://daisy.github.io/ace/) (EPUB), [Ace SMART by DAISY](https://smart.daisy.org/) (EPUB), [PAC Accessibility Checker](https://pac.pdf-accessibility.org/en) (PDF), and Adobe Acrobat (PDF). 

## Accessibility of this site
Our baseline accessibility target for this website is to meet WCAG 2.2 AA standards. 

The last accessibility evaluation for [leverpress.org](https://leverpress.org) was in February-March 2026.

Please see the [Fulcrum Accessibility page](https://www.fulcrum.org/accessibility) for accessibility information about the website and ebook platform at [fulcrum.org/leverpress](https://fulcrum.org/leverpress).

The next scheduled accessibility evaluation for this website is in 2027.
